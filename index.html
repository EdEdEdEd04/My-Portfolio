Record Management System

Capstone Project

Overview

Tourism Business Record Management System for Indang Tourism Office was developed with the aim of enhancing the management of tourism business records. It streamlines registration, record-keeping, and tourist arrival reporting by integrating these functions into a centralized data management system, resulting in improved accuracy and efficiency.

Components:

  • Admin Module

    Allows the administrator to access and manage the system, including account management, record management and report management.

  • Account Management

    This module allows the main administrator to create, modify and remove account information, assign permissions and access levels, and monitor account logs. Only the main administrator has the authority to register accounts for users and registered owner in order to secure and protect other modules from unauthorized access.

  • Registration Management

    It handles the process of registering new business and its owner. This module allows the sub admin 1 to register a tourism business by inputting all the information needed.

  • Tourist Arrival

    It handles the collection and consolidation of tourist arrival report from different registered owners. To consolidate all the files and summary of tourist arrival report, it allows the registered owners to submit their monthly tourist arrival report in excel file format using the section page provided for them and input the summary of tourist arrival report.

  • Report Management

    This module is capable of managing and printing of reports. It generates report such as registered tourism business report including the information of business and its owner, total number of registered tourism business, total number business for every business category and summary of tourist arrival report.
